Privacy Policy / Datenschutzerklärung

1. Privacy at a Glance

The operator of this website takes the protection of your personal data very seriously. We treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with the statutory data protection regulations (GDPR) and this privacy policy.

2. Data Collection on This Website

Cookies: This website does not use cookies. We do not track you.

Server Log Files: When you access our website, information is automatically processed by our hosting provider Cloudflare. This includes your IP address, browser type, and time of access. This is technically necessary to provide a stable and secure connection (Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR).

3. NTP Server Infrastructure

Our time server nodes (e.g., europe-central.smoothtime.io) are hosted at Hetzner Online GmbH in Germany. When your device synchronizes time via NTP, your IP address is processed to fulfill the request. We do not create long-term logs or profiles from this data.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to free information about your stored personal data at any time. You also have a right to correction or deletion of this data. Since we minimize data collection to the absolute technical minimum, we typically do not store any identifying personal data.
